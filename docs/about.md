## Motivation

I am passionate about developing AI systems that can learn from data while preserving user privacy. In today's data-driven world, the ability to train machine learning models without compromising sensitive information is crucial. My research focuses on privacy-preserving machine learning techniques, particularly homomorphic encryption and federated learning, to enable secure and private AI applications.
